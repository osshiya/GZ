Ships

You can build your own ship and renovate as you want with furnitures from shops and events!

Invite your friends to your ship and play with interactive furnitures!

Friends

Interact with friends online, send private messages and cute emoji!

Send each other dolls for collection, play missions together, or even spar one another!

You can even share your character slot and become twin!

Squads

=

Join squads with friends and dominate bases in Zone!

Form alliance with other squads to get help!

Dominate the bases to earn squad hours and become the top squads in the Squad Base Hour Scoreboard!

Get Cutomized Squad Rewads together with your squadmates as you hit certain squad hours!

Robots

You can build Autofire robots if you are a defender class and Medic robots if you are a medic class.

You can also buy pet robots and program them with simple codings!

Bring your pet robots to Bomb Spar and battle other players!

Rank up the Bomb Spar scoreboard and unlock the Reward Room for 1k Bombspar Wins for more cool pet robots!

PVP

Kill any players outside of the safe zone to gain kills!

Become the top player with the most kills in the Player Kiling Scores for Today, Weekly or even Total!

Get cool hats from the PK Reward Room as you hit certain amount of kills!

Spar

Head to Fight Club to spar other players!

There are practice spar for you to practice against other players.

Once you are confident enough, go to the Gun Spar or Melee Spar and build up your Sparring records!

Once you have certain amount of wins, go to the Spar Reward Room and get the honorable Spar hats!


Or you can even spar against other squads, work together in your team and eliminate other squad!

Rank top the Squad Spar Scoreboard!


Join the annual SST where all the top players come together and spar each other to be known as the top player of Zone!

Leave a mark of your victory in the SST room!


Become one of the top 3 players in either PK or Spar and get your own statue in the Fight Club entrance!

Staff

Become part of the Staff Team and help develop Zone!
